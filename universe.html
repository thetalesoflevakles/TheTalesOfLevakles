The Tales of Levakles is a book series that takes place on a futuristic version of earth thousands of years from now. The story takes place in, but is not limited to, Helios (the city in the sky), uncharted landscapes on the earth's surface, and underground tech dungeons. The vast majority of people live in Helios, which is the only truly safe place left on earth for humanity. On the ground, robotic beasts programmed to dispose of human life roam the earth in their attempt to fulfill their computerized function.

Now, society has learned to function very differently in their fight for survival. Banishment to the ground is considered to be a death sentence reserved explicitly for those who have committed the most horrendous of crimes. If the unpredictable landscape does not tap your mortality first, the beast bots will find you. And they WILL find you. But they may not harm you right away.

Instead, they might take you to Vexeral, an underground labyrinth enhanced by gravitech that was seemingly designed to trap, torture, and kill anyone brought inside. Of course, beast bots are familiar with the tunnels - they're the ones that built it. But why did they build it? What secrets does it hide? Perhaps YOU can be the one to find out!
