Technology has overtaken the world...

...and is hunting the human race.

Levakles, a world-renowned inventor-philosopher, discovered a successful method to isolate and manipulate the theorized graviton particle. Thanks to this advancement, other aspects of society also improved drastically; artificial intelligence evovled, nanotechnology revolutionized standard medical practices, and gravitech became an everyday tool. This technology made it possible for Helios to float in the sky and for the human race to survive The Glitch.

What's The Glitch, you ask? Simply put, it's the darkest day in human history; it was the day that the programming of certain technologies evolved machines into destructive mechanisms with the sole objective of eliminating human life. On the day of The Glitch, 70% of human life was eliminated. To this day, no one is entirely sure how it happened because Levakles - inventor of these corrupted technologies - vanished along with the vast majority of the rest of society. It's thought that Levakles' own machines killed him in the end, but that was years ago.

Now, survivors have lived three generations in the sky. Resources are limited and systems are failing in lower parts of the city. It is only a matter of time until society collapses and maker meets machine. Will humanity survive?
